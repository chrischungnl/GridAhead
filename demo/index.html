Methodology

Predictions come from a gradient-boosted regressor trained on roughly eight years of UK electricity market data: historical Agile rates, the half-hourly grid generation mix from Elexon BMRS, forecast carbon intensity, and weather ensembles for nine UK wind farms. Validation is five-fold GroupKFold by date — cross-validated R2 ≈ 0.88, MAE ≈ 1.9 p/kWh. A per-half-hour Kalman filter tracks residuals online and shifts each of the forty-eight daily slots when the market enters a new regime. After noon UK time, observed system settlement prices from Elexon are incorporated for same-day predictions. See METHODOLOGY.md for the full write-up.

Data feed

Raw predictions are available as static JSON on a Cloudflare R2 public bucket with CDN caching at Cloudflare’s edge. No authentication, no rate limits, no user-agent required. 

curl https://data.gridahead.chrischung.nl/predictions/C.json
  • /predictions/latest.json — all fourteen regions, next three days
  • /predictions/{A..P}.json — per-region files
  • /predictions/meta.json — forecast-run metadata and the regional multiplier table

Full schema and example responses are in the repository readme.
